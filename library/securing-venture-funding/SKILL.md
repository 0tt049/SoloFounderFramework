# Securing Venture Funding

## Goal
To audit venture capital pitch decks for structural story arcs, structure pitch-deck slides, formulate funding roadmaps, evaluate SAFE caps, and outline targeted investor profiles.

## When to Use This Skill
- When designing, outlining, or auditing pitch deck messaging structures and slide-by-slide narratives.
- When formulating fundraising strategies, SAFE cap discounts, and seed dilution projections.
- When defining targeted investor personas (VCs, angels, syndicates) and tracking warm outreach.

### Do Not Use This Skill For:
- Complex formal investment banking term-sheet modeling, general corporate finance, or listing compliance.

## Pitch Deck Narrative Arc & Workflow

```text
       Hook               Story & Traction               The Market                     The Ask
 ┌───────────────┐        ┌───────────────┐          ┌───────────────┐             ┌───────────────┐
 │ Vision, Team  │ ─────▻ │ Problem, Sol, │   ─────▻ │ TAM, Business │    ─────▻   │ Cap, Runway   │
 │ & Hook   (1)  │        │ Traction Proof│          │ Model, Compete│             │ Deployment (4)│
 └───────────────┘        └───────────────┘          └───────────────┘             └───────────────┘
```

1. **Phase 1: The Hook (Scoping & Vision):**
   - Grab attention immediately on the title/hook slide with a clear 1-sentence value proposition. Introduce founder-market fit early.
2. **Phase 2: Story & Traction Proof:**
   - Define a real, quantified customer problem and demonstrate a clear solution.
   - Present **Traction Indicators**: Customer growth, MRR, retention cohorts, beta feedback, or letters of intent (LOI).
3. **Phase 3: Market & Commercial Engine:**
   - Define Market Size: address TAM (Total Addressable Market), SAM, and SOM.
   - Explain the commercial engine: monetization plans, pricing channels, and competitor landscape comparison.
4. **Phase 4: Valuation & Capital Ask:**
   - Define the capital ask and match it directly to specific target milestone dates (e.g., "$1.5M over 18 months to reach $100K MRR").
   - Structure the raise using SAFE (Simple Agreements for Future Equity) and convertible note caps.

## Design Rules & Guidelines

### Pitch Deck Narrative Arc Rules
Reviews must audit and enforce an 11-slide sequence:
- **Slide 1**: Title / Value Prop Hook
- **Slide 2**: The Quantitative Problem
- **Slide 3**: The Solution & Demo
- **Slide 4**: Traction Proof / Key Milestones
- **Slide 5**: Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- **Slide 6**: Customer Acquisition / GTM
- **Slide 7**: Competitor Matrix (Differentiators)
- **Slide 8**: Business Model & Pricing
- **Slide 9**: The Team (Founder-Market Fit)
- **Slide 10**: Financial Projections (3-Year High Levels)
- **Slide 11**: The Ask & Use of Funds (Milestones-linked)

### Investor Targeting Strategy
- Formulate a targeted investor profile tracker based on check size, geography, vertical focus, and stage.
- Prioritize warm introductions. Prepare outbound email pitches kept under 120 words focusing on metrics over fluff.

### Seed Valuation & SAFE Rules
- Avoid stating single pre-money or post-money numbers as absolute guarantees. Always recommend standard SAFEs (valuation caps $\le$ pre-revenue norms, typically $5M-$10M depending on market, and optional $20\%$ discount triggers).
- Budget funding levels to sustain the company through 18-24 months of runway to allow adequate milestone achievement before next fundraising cycle.

## Example Implementation Checklist
Copy this checklist during funding advisory sessions:

- [ ] Select pitch deck slides checking for Problem, Solution, Team, and ask values.
- [ ] Ensure all 11 core narrative arc slides are addressed and properly structured.
- [ ] Highlight Traction Proof with hard metrics (LOIs, active users, organic signups).
- [ ] Link the financial Ask to concrete 18-24 month milestone dates and runway projections.
- [ ] Avoid rigid valuation numbers; structure the round via standard SAFE caps and discount parameters.
- [ ] Construct an investor target profile detailing fund parameters and matchmaking criteria.
- [ ] Deliver a concise, professional, constructive pitch deck feedback report.
