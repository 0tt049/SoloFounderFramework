# Viral Growth and Distribution

## Goal
To orchestrate high-leverage growth experiments, design organic viral loops, and write copy for non-paid marketing channels (Reddit, Twitter, Product Hunt).

## When to Use This Skill
- When planning a Go-to-Market (GTM) launch strategy on a limited or zero budget.
- When generating engaging, high-hook social media ad copywriting (under character bounds).
- When incorporating user-referral/incentive loops directly into a SaaS product.

### Do Not Use This Skill For:
- High-budget enterprise television campaigns or running extensive national multi-million dollar television commercials.

## Plan-Validate-Execute Workflow
1. **Plan (Target Channels & Hook Creation):**
   - Identify demographic hotspots: where does the target customer hang out (e.g., student subreddits for note apps, Hacker News for developer tools).
   - Define available launch capital (often $0 - $500).
2. **Validate (Assess Platform Rules):**
   - Respect platform-specific guidelines: ensure Reddit pitches conform to sub-reddit self-promotion constraints to avoid shadowbans.
   - Tailor copy formatting bounds: Twitter ad copies must be short (below 280 chars), high-hook, and leverage clear Call to Actions (CTAs).
3. **Execute (GTM & Copy Production):**
   - Provide a highly actionable, week-by-week 30-day GTM roadmap prioritizing organic growth.
   - Write multiple, punchy copy drafts with a hook + core value + CTA model.

## Design Rules & Guidelines

### The Organic-First Channel Guide
- **B2C student focus**: Prioritize micro-influencers, Student Campus Rep programs, organic TikToks, and SEO-driven study-tip guides.
- **B2B developer focus**: Prioritize technical documentations, Product Hunt launches, GitHub open-sourcing, and direct Twitter build-in-public hooks.

### Social Copy Layout Principles
- Hook must be in the first 50 characters.
- Use line breaks for readability. Use at most 2 emojis and 1 strategic hashtag.

## Example Implementation Checklist
Copy this checklist during Viral Growth tasks:

- [ ] Identify organic hotspots where target personas congregate.
- [ ] Establish GTM roadmap timelines with clear weekly actions.
- [ ] Draft engaging copy incorporating a strong, immediate Hook.
- [ ] Ensure copy adheres strictly to platform character and content boundaries.
- [ ] Incorporate structured CTAs in every landing page or social copy draft.
- [ ] Design at least one explicit viral mechanism (e.g., refer-a-friend credits, programmatic sharing).
